Our Mission

At Nema, we're standing to make a change in the world - creating a moonshot idea that will impact billions. Climate change is a very big problem in our world. By focusing on this, we're working towards creating a better future.

Our Vision

Nema has set a goal to eliminate the burning of plastic and to practice eco-friendly decomposition of plastic in recycling plants, then focus on the rest of the world.

Our Culture

At Nema, we practice the ability to put ourselves into a situation knowing full well that we might not know what to do. We trust our team to rise to the occasion, whatever the occasion. We accept that preparation is only important because it lends itself to making us more effective for game-time. #FigureItOut
